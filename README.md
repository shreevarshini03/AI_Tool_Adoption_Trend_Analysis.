# AI Tool Adoption Trend Analysis

## Overview

This project analyzes AI tool adoption trends across organizations and industries using statistical analysis and data visualization techniques. The objective is to identify adoption patterns, understand the impact of AI on productivity, and provide business recommendations based on data-driven insights.

## Problem Statement

Artificial Intelligence tools are transforming industries by improving productivity, automation, customer support, analytics, and software development. Organizations need to understand which AI tools are adopted the most, which industries lead AI adoption, and what factors influence adoption rates.

## Dataset

**Dataset Name:** ai_company_adoption.csv

The dataset contains organizational information related to:

* Industry
* Company Size
* AI Adoption Rate
* AI Tools Used
* Employee Satisfaction
* Productivity Change
* Employee Count
* AI Investment
* Business Performance Metrics

## Objectives

* Analyze AI adoption trends across industries.
* Identify the most commonly used AI tools.
* Study the relationship between company size and AI adoption.
* Measure the impact of AI adoption on productivity.
* Perform correlation analysis on key variables.
* Conduct hypothesis testing using ANOVA.

### Data Understanding

* Dataset inspection
* Missing value analysis
* Duplicate value checking

### Descriptive Statistics

* Mean
* Median

* Quartiles
* Percentiles

### Trend Analysis

* AI Tool Usage Analysis
* Industry-wise Adoption Analysis
* Company Size Analysis

### Correlation Analysis

* AI Adoption vs Productivity
* Employee Count vs AI Adoption
* Investment vs Performance

### Hypothesis Testing

**Null Hypothesis (H₀):** Company size has no impact on AI adoption.

**Alternative Hypothesis (H₁):** Company size significantly impacts AI adoption.

ANOVA testing was performed to evaluate the hypothesis.

## Key Findings

* AI adoption differs across industries.
* Some AI tools have significantly higher adoption rates.
* Higher AI adoption is associated with improved productivity.
* Company size influences AI adoption behavior.
* Positive relationships exist between AI adoption and business performance metrics.
